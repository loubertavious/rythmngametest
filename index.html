How to Play:

Player 1 - Recording

  • Click "Start Recording"
  • Press arrow keys (← ↓ ↑ →) to create your rhythm
  • Click "Stop" when finished
  • Your pattern will be saved

Player 2 - Playback

  • Click "Start Playback"
  • Watch the notes scroll down
  • Press the same arrow keys when notes hit the bottom
  • Match the rhythm to score points!
